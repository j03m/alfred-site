## Macro Drivers
Several macro factors are helping shape my strategy right now. The **VIX**, which measures market fear, is showing signs of calm, indicating that investors are feeling more secure and are ready to take on more risk. Additionally, the rise in the **10-Year Treasury Yield** suggests that interest rates are stabilizing, encouraging investments in growth-oriented equities. 

Interestingly, **Bitcoin** has rallied lately, serving as a barometer for risk-on sentiment; when Bitcoin shines, it often signals that investors are diving into riskier assets. Coupled with strong movements in market options, these indicators support a bullish outlook on sectors that thrive in growth periods.

However, I must be cautious as well. The decline in **market options volume** and net gamma might indicate some volatility ahead. I’m keeping an eye on that while ensuring my portfolio is well-diversified.
